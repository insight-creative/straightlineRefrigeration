Cranberry Freezer

A custom-built, 175,000 square foot cranberry freezer that houses up to 3,000,000 pounds of cranberries.

Project Overview

175,000 sq.ft 3,000,000 lbs of cranberries All installed piping and fittings were 304 stainless steel Customized Evaporators to increase cooling and reduce defrost times 2 stage system with compressors matched for harvest load and also year round cold storage load 108” Low Temp Recirculator package built at Straightline Refrigeration’s shop Evaporative Condenser sized for harvest load as well as able to meet load reduction during harsh winters Kept valves accessible even with changing roof heights Cranberries arrive hot and wet directly from field washing and go into quick chill freezer to increase product life Project onsite time was 4 months Utilized shop fabrication to meet customer schedule and accommodate weather conditions
